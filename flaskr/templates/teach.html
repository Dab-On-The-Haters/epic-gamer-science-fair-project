Teaching with Joe



Datasets

To teach the neural networks English, Joe requires a large amount of text files. These textfiles are compiled into a dataset

You can upload your own datasets at this page.

Explore previously uploaded datasets here


Not sure where to look for data? Some good places to get datasets from are Kaggle, a data science site, and Project Gutenberg, a collection of books with expired copyright. Some examples of fun datasets are food reviews, book series', tweets, and scripts.



Models

AI data is stored and generated in instances called "models". These models are essentially what Joe is.

You can customize and train your own models using your site at this page.
